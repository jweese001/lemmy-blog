# Lemmy's Mic - Illustration Style Guide

## Core Visual Language

Every hero image uses these techniques (NOT photorealistic):

- **Heavy ink linework** - Bold black outlines, hand-drawn feel
- **Chiaroscuro** - High contrast light/shadow, deep blacks, stark highlights
- **Cel-shaded shadows** - Graphic shapes, not soft gradients
- **Selective color** - Mostly muted/desaturated palette with occasional saturated pops to accent the subject
- **Edge-to-edge composition** - No white borders, fills the frame
- **Comic book aesthetic** - Illustrated, not photographed

## What to Avoid

- Photorealism / "professional photography feel"
- Soft, airbrushed gradients
- Fedoras, 1940s detective imagery, film noir clichés
- Generic AI aesthetic (over-rendered, plastic-looking)
- Stock photo compositions

## Artist Influences

When generating, pick an influence that fits the subject. Note which artist inspired the piece.

| Artist | Known For | Good For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Frank Miller | Heavy blacks, stark contrast, graphic violence | Dark/gritty subjects, urban scenes |
| Moebius (Jean Giraud) | Clean lines, epic scope, surreal color, sci-fi | Tech, expansive landscapes, futurism |
| Frank Frazetta | Dynamic figures, dramatic lighting, painterly | Action, heroic subjects, fantasy |
| Jack Kirby | Bold shapes, cosmic energy, Kirby Krackle, dynamism | Tech, energy, larger-than-life subjects |
| George Pérez | Detailed, dynamic composition, crowd scenes | Complex scenes, multiple subjects |
| Los Bros Hernandez | Strong blacks, indie sensibility, everyday life | Punk, community, slice-of-life |
| Dave McKean | Mixed media, painterly texture, abstract | Moody, experimental, artistic subjects |
| Mike Mignola | Simplified shapes, heavy shadows, folklore | Dark subjects, supernatural, stark |
| Geof Darrow | Insane detail, mechanical complexity | Tech, machines, intricate subjects |
| Paul Pope | Energetic linework, fashion-forward, urban | Youth culture, urban, punk |

## Per-Topic Defaults

| Topic | Primary Influence | Palette Notes |
|-------|-------------------|---------------|
| Indie Games | Kirby, Moebius | Neon pops (cyan, magenta) against dark |
| E-Bikes | Moebius, Geof Darrow | Urban grays with accent color |
| Punk Music | Los Bros Hernandez, Paul Pope | High contrast B&W with earth tone pops |
| Kava Bars | Dave McKean, Moebius | Warm amber/ochre, relaxed palette |
| Tech | Kirby, Moebius | Cool blues, digital accents |

## Usage

When generating a hero image:
1. Pick 1-2 artists from the list that fit the subject
2. Include their influence in the style prompt
3. Note which artist(s) inspired the final image

Example prompt fragment:
> "Heavy ink illustration influenced by Moebius. Bold outlines, high contrast chiaroscuro, muted palette with selective cyan accents..."
